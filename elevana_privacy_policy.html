Elevana – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 18, 2025
Developer: İbrahim Atmaca

Introduction

At Elevana, we value your privacy. Our intention is to help you stay focused and aligned through your personal goals — without tracking or collecting your personal data. This policy explains what data we do and do not collect, and how your information is handled.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact details
  • We do not collect your device identifiers or location
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
  • We do not send any of your data to remote servers

What We Store (Locally)

Elevana only stores your intention texts (goals) locally on your device using Apple’s secure UserDefaults and optionally displays them on your lock screen using WidgetKit.

Your data never leaves your device, unless you manually back it up using iCloud or Apple’s native backup systems.

Internet Access

Elevana does not require an internet connection for its core functionality. There is no in-app web browser, external API access, or online syncing feature. However, an internet connection may be required for in-app purchases through Apple's StoreKit.

In-App Purchases and StoreKit

Elevana uses Apple's StoreKit framework to offer in-app purchases and premium features. When you make a purchase:

  • Payment Processing: All payment transactions are processed entirely by Apple through their secure payment system. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or billing address.
  • Purchase Data: Apple handles and stores all purchase-related data, including transaction history and receipts. This information is managed according to Apple's privacy policy and terms of service.
  • Local Purchase Status: Elevana may store your purchase status locally on your device (e.g., whether premium features are unlocked) to enable or disable features accordingly. This information is stored securely using Apple's UserDefaults and is not transmitted to any external servers.
  • Restore Purchases: If you restore purchases, Elevana uses Apple's StoreKit to verify your purchase history with Apple's servers. This verification process is handled entirely by Apple, and we do not receive or store any personal information during this process.

For more information about how Apple handles your payment and purchase data, please review Apple's Privacy Policy. Elevana is licensed to you under Apple's Standard End User License Agreement, which governs your use of the application.

Children's Privacy

Elevana does not collect any data from users and is suitable for children aged 4 and above under Apple’s App Store guidelines.
