Elevana – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 18, 2025
Developer: İbrahim Atmaca

Introduction

At Elevana, we value your privacy. Our intention is to help you stay focused and aligned through your personal goals — without tracking or collecting your personal data. This policy explains what data we do and do not collect, and how your information is handled.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact details
  • We do not collect your device identifiers or location
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
  • We do not send any of your data to remote servers

What We Store (Locally)

Elevana only stores your intention texts (goals) locally on your device using Apple’s secure UserDefaults and optionally displays them on your lock screen using WidgetKit.

Your data never leaves your device, unless you manually back it up using iCloud or Apple’s native backup systems.

Internet Access

Elevana does not require an internet connection. There is no in-app web browser, external API access, or online syncing feature.

Children's Privacy

Elevana does not collect any data from users and is suitable for children aged 4 and above under Apple’s App Store guidelines.
